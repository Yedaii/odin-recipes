Pizza

Description

Tortilla

Ingredients

  • 360g flour
  • 6g salt
  • 7,5g baking powder
  • 240ml boiling water
  • 80ml melted butter

Steps

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients.
  2. Add the boiling water.
  3. Add the melted butter.
  4. Mix until the dough becomes homogeneous.
  5. Rest the dough for about 15 minutes, covered.
  6. Divide the tortillas, and flatten them on a wooden board with flower.
  7. Bake them on a very hot pan for about 40 seconds each side, or until slightly charred.
