Pizza

Description

Easy brigadeiro recipe with just 3 ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 400g cream 17% fat
  • 395g condensed milk
  • 60g cocoa powder 100% or 75%

Steps

  1. Add the cream to the pot with the cocoa powder, and mix well until homogeneous.
  2. Add the condensed milk, and mix well
  3. Turn on the stove, and dont stop mixing and scraping the bottom of the pot.
  4. When the mix turns a lot thicker, turn off the stove and keep mixing for a few more minutes.
  5. Transfer the mix to a bowl, cover it with plastic film, and let it rest in room temperature until warm.
  6. Take the bowl to the fridge, and let it rest for 24 hours or until firm.
  7. Spread butter on hands, use a spoon to scoop some of the mix, and shape into little balls.
  8. Place the now brigadeiro on chocolate sprinkles, and roll them around until totally covered.
